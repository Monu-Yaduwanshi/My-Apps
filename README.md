# ⚠️ Fix Installation Warning (Important)

If you see warning like *"App not installed"* or *"Blocked by Play Protect"*, follow below steps:

---

## 🔹 Disable Play Protect

1. Open **Google Play Store**
2. Tap on your **Profile Icon (top right)**
3. Click on **Manage apps & device**
4. Tap on **No harmful apps found**
5. Click on **Settings (⚙️ icon)**
6. Turn OFF both options:
   - ❌ Scan apps with Play Protect  
   - ❌ Improve harmful app detection  

---

## 🔹 Allow Unknown Apps Installation

1. Open **Mobile Settings**
2. Go to **Security / Privacy**
3. Click on **Install Unknown Apps**
4. Select your browser or file manager  
5. Enable **Allow from this source**
